Open Budgets: New Policy Proposals - Vanuatu's Spending Priorities

In practice for the Vanuatu government, New Policy Proposals (NPPs) represent the main changes to spending within a budget. The Ministerial Budget Committee deliberate over these priorities for the coming year, some of which will be longer-term (recurrent) commitments, some will be one-off activities.

If you want to know what main spending decisions were made in the last three budgets as stated by the government, this is a good place to start. Understanding, and sometimes questioning, how these decisions fit with development plans is central to engaging, influencing and holding account those responsible for managing public funds.

To use this resource, hover over each of the pie slices to see the department name and the amount of new money being allocated to it. Click on the pie slice to see a detailed list of new policy proposals associated with that department.
